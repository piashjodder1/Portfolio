Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical tech stack?
I heavily rely on React/Next.js for the frontend, styled with Tailwind CSS and Shadcn UI principles. For backend logic, I use Node.js or Laravel, connected to PostgreSQL/MySQL, usually deployed via Vercel and AWS.
Do you design before coding?
Yes! I believe in visualizing the product first. I create wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma to ensure we agree on the UI/UX direction before writing a single line of code.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Absolutely. I offer monthly retainer packages for maintenance, server monitoring, bug fixes, and feature additions to ensure your web app runs smoothly post-launch.
