Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process for a new project?
My process is highly structured: Discovery Call -> Strategy & Proposal -> UI/UX Design (Figma) -> Client Approval -> Agile Development (Next.js/Laravel) -> QA Testing -> Launch & Support. I ensure complete transparency at every stage.
Do you only do frontend or full-stack?
I am a dedicated Full-Stack Developer. I handle everything from database architecture (MySQL, PostgreSQL) and server logic (PHP/Laravel) to premium frontend interfaces (React/Next.js). I build complete, production-ready systems.
How do you handle revisions?
During the UI/UX design phase, I offer extensive revisions to ensure the visual direction is perfect. Once the design is approved and development begins, technical revisions are scoped according to the project contract to ensure timely delivery.
