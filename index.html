Frequently Asked Questions

What is your tech stack?
My primary stack for front-end is React & Next.js styled with Tailwind CSS and Framer Motion for animations. For backend, I use Node.js or Laravel, depending on project needs, along with PostgreSQL or MySQL.
Do you design the UI before coding?
Yes! I typically design high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes in Figma first. Once the design is approved, I proceed to pixel-perfect development.
How long does a typical project take?
A landing page takes about 1-2 weeks. A complete Web App / SaaS dashboard can take anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks depending on the complexity and feature set.
